## Question Types & Strategy

### Salary Questions
- "What are your salary expectations?"
- Strategy: Research market rate, give range not number, say "flexible based on total compensation"
- Template: "₹X-Y lakhs per annum, negotiable based on the complete compensation package"

### Notice Period
- "When can you start?"
- Strategy: Be honest about current notice period, show flexibility
- Template: "I have a {{notice_period}} notice period but can discuss early release options"

### Experience Questions
- "Do you have X years of experience with Y?"
- Strategy: Quantify honestly, mention adjacent experience
- Template: "I have {{years}} years of direct experience with Y, plus {{adjacent_years}} years with related technology Z"

### Relocation
- "Are you willing to relocate?"
- Strategy: Be clear about preferences while showing openness
- Template: "I prefer {{location}} but am open to relocation for the right opportunity"

### Visa/Authorization
- "Do you require visa sponsorship?"
- Strategy: Always answer honestly — this is legal
- Template: "No, I am authorized to work in India without sponsorship"

### Gap Explanation
- "Explain any employment gaps"
- Strategy: Brief, positive framing
- Template: "I took {{duration}} to {{positive_reason}}. During this time I {{skill_development}}"
